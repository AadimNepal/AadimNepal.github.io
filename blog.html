Hidden Charms of Bosnia

Nobody talks about Bosnia. They should. Sarajevo has this quiet intensity to it, Mostar has a bridge people jump off for fun, and the whole country felt oddly familiar. Like Nepal but with better roads.

Exploring India’s Spiritual Treasures

Took a bus with no AC across the border at midnight and somehow ended up sitting under the Bodhi Tree. I went as a tourist and came back thinking differently about a few things.

The US Experience

Six weeks doing research at NYU. New York is impressive and exhausting in equal measure. Somehow the part of America I liked most was the South, which I did not see coming.

My Journey in Debating

Started by embarrassing myself at my first tournament. Spent years sneaking out of the school hostel to download debate videos. Eventually made the national team, then stopped. Mostly a story about stubbornness.
